Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Darshan ("we," "us," or "this site") collects, uses, and discloses information when you visit dailydarshan.online. We built this site to be as lightweight as possible — there is no user account system and no database storing personal information — but it's still important to be transparent about the data that is collected, especially by third-party services we rely on.

Information we collect directly

Daily Darshan does not require you to create an account, log in, or submit any personal information to use the core features of the site (lighting a diya, reading verses, sharing a shrine). Any "offering" activity you perform is stored only temporarily in your browser's memory for the duration of your visit and is not saved to any server or database.

Cookies and similar technologies

This site, and the third-party services embedded in it (described below), may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to operate, measure performance, and serve advertising. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; please note that disabling cookies may affect how parts of this site function.

Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.
  • For more detail on how Google uses information from sites that use its services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Analytics

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use this site — for example, which shrines are visited most often, or which pages need improvement. These services may use cookies and collect information such as your approximate location (derived from IP address), device type, and browsing behaviour on this site. This information is aggregated and is not used to personally identify you.

Children's privacy

Daily Darshan is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies, though doing so may limit certain features of this site or other sites you visit.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.

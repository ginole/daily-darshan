Daily Darshan

Light a diya. Offer your prayer. Carry peace with you.

Daily Darshan is a quiet space to begin or close your day — nine shrines, nine traditions of devotion, open whenever you need a moment of stillness. Light a diya, count a japa mala, and read today’s verse.
